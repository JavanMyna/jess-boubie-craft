Our History & Contact

Our Story

Jess Boubie Craft is a homemade artisan business started in 2017. All crafts are made by one artisan.

In some Dusun traditions, brass bracelets are associated with cultural beliefs and practices, including the concept of "Buang Angin". These traditions continue to inspire many of our handcrafted designs today.
